<p><ul><li>Cold Rolling</li><li>Hot Rolling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Rolling Oil market is segmented into two main types: cold rolling and hot rolling. Cold rolling involves the processing of aluminium at room temperature, enhancing its strength and surface finish, while maintaining dimensional accuracy. This method is typically used for producing thin sheets and foils. Hot rolling, on the other hand, involves heating aluminium to high temperatures, allowing it to be easily shaped and processed into thicker sheets and plates. Both types require specific rolling oils to optimize performance and minimize defects.</p></p>
