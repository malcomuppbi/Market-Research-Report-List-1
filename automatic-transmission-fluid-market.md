<p><ul><li>Semi-Synthetic Automatic Transmission Fluid</li><li>Full-Synthetic Automatic Transmission Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic transmission fluid market is divided into two main types: semi-synthetic and full-synthetic fluids. Semi-synthetic automatic transmission fluid combines mineral oil with synthetic additives, providing enhanced performance and better protection than conventional fluids. It offers improved thermal stability and viscosity characteristics, making it suitable for a wide range of vehicles. In contrast, full-synthetic automatic transmission fluid is entirely synthetic, delivering superior lubrication, enhanced wear protection, and greater resistance to breakdown at high temperatures, making it ideal for high-performance and modern vehicles.</p></p>
